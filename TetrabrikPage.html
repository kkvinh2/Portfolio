Ready to Drink; Fall 2022 Collection

Iris van Herpen is a Dutch designer at the forefront of fashion and technology. She was the first to put a 3D printed dress on the runway, challenging both worlds to understand the power of interdisciplinary art. The process is done through the creation of precise digital models that are then sent to a Stereolithographic printer. The Printer creates the shapes in resin by passing a UV light over each layer resulting in a clear ice like appearance.

This Ice Tetra Brick was created using the same methods. The shape was designed on Rhino to allow and reflect light selectively as to give it the qualities of frozen water. It was printed in 5 separate pieces and glued together then sealed along the edges that needed to be translucent.
