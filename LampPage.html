Kiku No Hana Lamp

The Japanese handcrafted Kiku No Hana lamp was launched in 2016 by Time and Style.Our goal was to redesign this beautiful classic lamp. We used natural and recycled materials to embrace the idea of Japanese design aesthetic. Each piece of the structure was carefully designed so that the lamp fits together like a 3d puzzle. The paper shade uses the beautiful leaves from the fall season in Segovia. We ironed the leaf patterns in between the wax sheets and attached them between the CNC cut wooden structure.
